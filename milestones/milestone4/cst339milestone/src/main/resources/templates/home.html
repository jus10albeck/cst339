Welcome to the Movie Library!

Why a Physical Movie Library?

Within the last year, my wife and I have been building a collection of our favorite movies.

We chose to start the collection for three reasons:
1.
We were sick of having to jump between 5 different streaming services to search for a movie that might not be available on any of them.

2.
We want to know what our future children are watching, and the best way to do that is to avoid streaming services and build a library of a variety of movies that uphold our values.

3.
Multiple streaming services are becoming too expensive, and prices just keep rising! Additionally, the pros of cheap, ad-free streaming or "password sharing" are beginning to disappear across platforms.

While it is fun stumbling upon a movie to add to the collection out in the wild, sometimes it is hard to keep track of what movies you own or are still searching for.

This is where this application comes in handy.

This application allows you to keep track of your movie collection, keeping track of movies you are still searching for, and an overall watch list of movies you have seen!.
